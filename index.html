How to Use ExamPath 2027

🎓 What is ExamPath 2027?

A free, offline-capable exam and career navigation agent for Grade 12 PCM students (Batch of 2027). Covers 53 exams across engineering, research, design, management, defence, and international options.

No login. No data collection. No internet needed after loading. Progress saves locally in your browser.

📋 How to use each section

  • All Exams: Browse 53 exams. Filter by track. The urgency ring shows days until registration closes. Click 🔗 to open the official website. Update status via the dropdown — saves automatically.
  • Timeline: Month-by-month from Jul 2026 to Sep 2027. Click a month header to expand.
  • Career Compass: 5 questions → personalised career match + priority exam list.
  • My Progress: Every exam in a table. Update status and track completion.
  • Why Each Career: 9 careers evaluated — India need, ROI, impact. Click to expand.

⭐ The Hidden Gem: IISc Bangalore

IISc Bangalore is NIRF #1 in India — right in Bangalore. Admits via IAT exam (June). Fees: ~₹15,000/year. INSPIRE scholarship: ₹80,000/year — net cost nearly zero. Register at both iiseradmission.in AND admissions.iisc.ac.in separately. PCM students need basic Biology preparation.

🛡️ Defence Shortcut: Army TES + Navy BTech

Both Army Technical Entry Scheme and Navy 10+2 BTech Cadet Entry use JEE Main rank as sole shortlisting criterion. No extra exam. No fee. Apply after JEE Main 2027 result — 30 minutes. Zero extra preparation needed.

🟢 BLR Tag

Cards marked BLR have a campus or key resource in Bangalore. 14 options including IISc (NIRF #1), IIIT Bangalore, PES, RV College, BMS College, Amrita Bengaluru, Scaler SST, NID Bengaluru, NIFT Bangalore, Srishti Manipal, Azim Premji University, Liverpool Bengaluru.

⚠️ Note on Dates

All 2027 exam dates are estimated based on 2025–26 patterns. Always verify on official websites before registering or paying fees.
